# Colophon

These are the projects this site is built upon.

## Technology

- **Static site generator:** [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/)
- **CSS framework:** [system7.css](https://sakofchit.github.io/system.css/) by sakofchit
- **Hosting:** [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/)
- **Source code:** [github.com/abbyfluoroethane](https://github.com/abbyfluoroethane/abbyfluoroethane.github.io)

## Design

The site simulates a macOS System 7 desktop environment. The entire content area is rendered inside a single window, complete with a functional menu bar, title bar controls, and scrollable content pane.

## Themes

Four color themes are available from the View menu:

- **Monochrome Light** — Classic black-on-white, faithful to the original Macintosh
- **Monochrome Dark** — Inverted for late-night computing
- **Catppuccin Latte** — Warm pastel light theme with pink accents
- **Catppuccin Mocha** — Cozy dark theme with pink accents

## Typography

- **UI text:** Chicago
- **Body text:** JetBrains Mono
- **Accessible option:** Atkinson Hyperlegible, available from the View menu
